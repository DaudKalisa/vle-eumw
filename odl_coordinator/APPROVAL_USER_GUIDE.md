# Claim Approval with Signature - Quick Start Guide

## What's New?
You can now approve lecturer finance claims directly from the claim details page and add your digital signature as proof of approval.

## Where to Find It?
1. Open a lecturer's claim in the print claim page
2. Look for the **blue "✓ Approve"** button in the top toolbar
3. The button appears only if your role should approve at this stage

## Step-by-Step: How to Approve & Sign

### Step 1: Click the Approve Button
- **ODL Coordinator**: Click "✓ Approve (ODL)"
- **Dean**: Click "✓ Approve (Dean)" 
- **Finance Officer**: Click "✓ Approve (Finance)"

### Step 2: The Signature Dialog Opens
You'll see a modal with TWO options for providing your signature

### Step 3A: OPTION A - Draw Your Signature
```
1. Click the "✏️ Draw Signature" tab
2. Use your mouse/touchpad/stylus to sign
3. Draw in the white box below the tabs
4. If you make a mistake, click "Clear" and try again
5. When satisfied, click "Use This" to save your drawing
```

### Step 3B: OPTION B - Upload a Signature Image
```
1. Click the "📤 Upload Signature" tab
2. Click "Choose File" or drag & drop
3. Select a PNG or JPG image of your signature (max 2MB)
4. Preview the image below the upload area
5. If you want to change it, click "Change File"
```

### Step 4: Add Remarks (Optional)
- Write any notes like:
  - "Approved with conditions"
  - "All documentation verified"
  - "Finance to process payment"

### Step 5: Confirm
- ☑ Check the box: "I confirm that this signature is authentic..."
- The **Submit Approval** button becomes clickable

### Step 6: Submit
- Click **Submit Approval**
- You'll see a loading spinner
- The claim page reloads automatically

## What Happens After Approval?

The claim moves through multiple approval stages:

```
┌─────────────────────────────────────┐
│ STAGE 1: ODL Coordinator             │
│ Reviews claim and signs              │
├─────────────────────────────────────┤
│ STAGE 2: Dean of Faculty             │
│ Reviews and countersigns             │
├─────────────────────────────────────┤
│ STAGE 3: Finance Officer             │
│ Final approval for payment           │
└─────────────────────────────────────┘
```

## Signature Visibility

Your approved status appears as:
- **Green Badge**: ✓ APPROVED (with date)
- **Yellow Badge**: PENDING (waiting for next approver)

Your signature image displays in the signature section of the form (visible when printed).

## Things to Know

✓ Your signature is automatically saved  
✓ The system records WHO approved and WHEN  
✓ Remarks are stored for audit trail  
✓ You can only approve at your designated level  
✓ Once approved, only admin can change it  

## Troubleshooting

**"Submit Approval" button is gray/disabled**
- → You need to provide a signature first (draw or upload)
- → You need to check the confirmation box

**Drawing doesn't appear**
- → Make sure you're in the "✏️ Draw" tab
- → Try refreshing the page and opening the modal again

**File upload fails**
- → Check the file is PNG or JPG
- → Make sure file is under 2MB
- → Try uploading a different image

**Signature not saved after approval**
- → Check if you got a success message
- → The page should reload automatically
- → If not, manually refresh to see the update

## Security Reminder

✅ Only use your own signature  
✅ Review claim details before approving  
✅ Add remarks explaining your approval decision  
✅ The system logs all approvals for audit purposes  

## Need Help?

3. Contact your system administrator
