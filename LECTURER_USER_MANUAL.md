# Lecturer User Manual

This guide helps lecturers use the Virtual Learning Environment (VLE) system. Each section includes step-by-step instructions and screenshot placeholders.

---

## 1. Logging In

- Go to the VLE login page.
- Enter your lecturer username and password.
- Click **Login**.

![Screenshot: Lecturer Login Page](assets/screenshots/lecturer_login.png)

---

## 2. Lecturer Dashboard Overview

- After login, you see the dashboard with navigation links for courses, assignments, students, and messages.

![Screenshot: Lecturer Dashboard](assets/screenshots/lecturer_dashboard.png)

---

## 3. Viewing Courses

- Click **Courses** in the dashboard menu.
- See a list of your assigned courses.

![Screenshot: Courses List](assets/screenshots/lecturer_courses.png)

---

## 4. Managing Assignments

- Click **Assignments**.
- View, create, or edit assignments for your courses.
- To add a new assignment:
  - Click **Add Assignment**.
  - Fill in the details (title, description, due date).
  - Click **Save**.

![Screenshot: Assignment Management](assets/screenshots/lecturer_assignments.png)

---

## 5. Reviewing Student Submissions

- Click **Submissions** under Assignments.
- See submitted work for each assignment.
- Click a student name to view their submission.
- Download files or add feedback.

![Screenshot: Student Submissions](assets/screenshots/lecturer_submissions.png)

---

## 6. Grading Assignments

- In the Submissions view, enter marks and comments.
- Click **Submit Grade** to save.

![Screenshot: Grading Interface](assets/screenshots/lecturer_grading.png)

---

## 7. Messaging Students

- Click **Messages** in the dashboard.
- Send announcements or private messages to students.

![Screenshot: Messaging Students](assets/screenshots/lecturer_messages.png)

---

## 8. Accessing Finance/Payment Info

- If enabled, click **Finance** to view fee status for students.
- See which students have paid or are pending.

![Screenshot: Finance Overview](assets/screenshots/lecturer_finance.png)

---

## 9. Logging Out

- Click **Logout** in the top menu to exit securely.

![Screenshot: Logout Button](assets/screenshots/lecturer_logout.png)

---

## Tips
- Use the navigation menu for quick access.
- Check notifications for important updates.
- Contact admin if you have login or access issues.
